About me

Economist, journalist, mentor, and community builder — making things happen at UBC Okanagan and beyond.

"I thrive at the intersection of leadership, storytelling, and strategic thinking."

I'm Samaira Talwar — a third-year Economics student at UBC Okanagan with a Pre-Law focus and a long track record of actually doing things. I don't wait to have the title before stepping up.

At The Phoenix News, I started as a Social Media Manager in 2024, grew their audience by 70%, moved into Operations, started writing, and by 2026 I was Editor-in-Chief — managing the full editorial cycle for a real publication read across campus.

My instinct is always toward: What does this community need, and how do I build it? Whether that's an editorial calendar, a mentorship program, or a conflict resolution conversation — I figure it out.
